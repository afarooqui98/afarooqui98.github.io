Week 1

The first week was quite slow as onboarding usually is, but I didn’t know that until somebody told me. I felt a huge amount of FOMO on all sides. I had no idea what I was exactly doing, and for some reason my laptop was not configured properly so setting up the server instance took way longer than it should have. I’m very apprehensive about this and I have horrible imposter syndrome. All the other interns are big goys from Stanford or UCLA. I feel like a little baby that’s wandered into an office space.
